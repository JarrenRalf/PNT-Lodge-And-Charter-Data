1. Login to Adagio Sales Analysis
  2. Click Inquiry
  3. Double click Lodge Sales YTD Summary with INV #
  4. Click on Quantity Specif
    1. Below in the select date range section, change the Starting range to be January 1st of the desired year
    2. Change the Ending range to be December 31st of the desired year
  5. Click on Amount Specific
    1. Below in the select date range section, change the Starting range to be January 1st of the desired year
    2. Change the Ending range to be December 31st of the desired year
  6. Click View
  7. Press the Excel icon button
  8. Save the data
  9. Import the data into this spreadsheet by clicking File → Import
